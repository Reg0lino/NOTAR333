At a Glance: Current & Pending Rules (June 2025)

✔

Mandatory Notary Journal

Effective Law (Jan 25, 2023)

All notaries (traditional and electronic) must maintain a detailed journal of all notarial acts. Records must be kept for 10 years.

✔

Electronic Notarization (REN)

Effective Law (Jan 25, 2023)

Authorized with specific registration, compliant tech (credential analysis, identity proofing), and 10-year AV recording retention.

✖️

Remote Ink Notarization (RIN)

Prohibited (Jan 31, 2023)

Remote Ink Notarization (RIN) is no longer permitted in NYS. Only fully electronic remote notarization is sanctioned.

⏳

S.B. S398: Real Property Rules

Pending Legislation

Proposes new duties for residential real property conveyances, including inked thumbprints in journals and a detailed "colloquy form".

⏳

S.B. S6268: Fee Increase

Pending Legislation

Proposes increasing standard notarial fees for acknowledgments and oaths from the current $2.00 to $5.00 per act.
